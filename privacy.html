Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 December 2025

Who We Are

A&J Gutter Cleaning is a professional exterior cleaning service operating in the Hawkesbury region of NSW, Australia. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your message or enquiry details
  • Property address (if provided)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Provide quotes for our services
  • Schedule and deliver our services
  • Communicate about your booking or service

We do not use your information for marketing purposes unless you explicitly request to receive updates from us.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form handling service. When you submit the contact form, your information is transmitted through Formspree's servers before being forwarded to us via email. You can review Formspree's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

Our website is hosted on CloudFlare Pages. CloudFlare may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses for security and performance purposes. See CloudFlare's Privacy Policy for more information.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, and disclosure. Your form submissions are transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.
