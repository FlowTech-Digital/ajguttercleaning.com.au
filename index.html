What We Do

A&J is your one-stop destination for all your exterior cleaning needs. We specialise in comprehensive exterior cleaning services that keep your home or business looking its best—while protecting it from costly damage.

Gutter Cleaning

Prevent water damage and protect your property with our thorough gutter cleaning service. We remove leaves, debris, and blockages to ensure proper drainage.

  • Full debris removal
  • Downpipe flushing
  • Before & after photos
  • Roof inspection included

Chimney Sweeping

Keep your chimney safe and efficient with professional sweeping. Essential maintenance for wood fire season preparation.

  • Creosote removal
  • Safety inspection
  • Flue cleaning
  • Cap inspection

Fireplace Installs & Maintenance

Every installation is done to code, ensuring safe and efficient venting for fireplaces. Adin has completed the AHHA Certified Installer Program.

  • Code-compliant installation
  • Safe venting systems
  • Ongoing maintenance
  • Safety certification

Pressure Washing

Restore your driveways, pathways, and exterior surfaces to their original condition with our high-pressure cleaning service.

  • Driveways & paths
  • Concrete cleaning
  • Fence restoration
  • Commercial surfaces

Soft Washing

Gentle yet effective cleaning for delicate surfaces. Perfect for roofs, rendered walls, and facades that need careful treatment.

  • Roof cleaning
  • Render & facades
  • Low-pressure safe
  • Algae & mould removal
